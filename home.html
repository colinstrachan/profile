My Favourite Cities

Montréal, Canada

Montréal is the second most-populous city in Canada and the principal metropolis of the province of Quebec. It is the largest French-speaking city in the world after Paris.

Buenos Aires, Argentina

Buenos Aires is the capital and largest city of Argentina. The city is located on the western shore of the estuary of the Río de la Plata, on the South American continent's southeastern coast.

Manchester, U.K.

Manchester is the third-most visited city in the UK, after London and Edinburgh. It is notable for its architecture, culture, musical exports, media links, scientific and engineering output, social impact, sports clubs and transport connections.
